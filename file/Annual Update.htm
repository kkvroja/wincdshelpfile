Annual Update-Quarterly Sales

When your PHYSICAL YEAR ENDS, you need to update the Quarterly sales as displayed on the inventory screen.  This will purge last years sales figures, by quarters, and move the current years sales figures into last years positions.

Note: Before you run this, you may want to print the following reports which are based on these sales figures.  This would be a good timer to perform a POS Backup. 

  • Inventory, Reports, Inventory Reports, Quarterly Inventory Sales Comparison Report     

  • Inventory, Reports, Best Sellers

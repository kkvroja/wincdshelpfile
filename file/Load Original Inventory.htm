Loading Original Inventory

You can load your original inventory in one of two ways:
  • Enter Quantities Manually
  • Import From Barcode Scanner
Both of these will update your original quantities to reflect your current levels.


Enter Quantities Manually

If you select Enter Quantities Manually, Click this to load the original inventory from your Physical Inventory Worksheets.

Select each vendors in the drop down. The software will automatically bring up every inventory item in the order of your physical work sheet by vendor. Click the Ok button. All the items from that vendor will automatically come up on the screen.

To add Inventory, put the quantity in the quantity box and also, distribute the quantity in each location. The allocation figure will automatically increase by the quantity you put in each location. The sum of each location must match the Quantity. Be sure to click the Apply button for each item. Click the Next > button to move to the next item for that vendor and repeat the process for each vendor.

Import From Barcode Scanner

If you have purchased our Data Collector, you can automatically enter your inventory.

Before you begin, the you will need barcodes for each of your items.
While you are entering the basic inventory information (style, manufacturer, description, etc) print out a retail price tag for the floor. These tags will have barcodes for all your floor stock.

Once you have barcodes, you should:
  • Set the scanner to receive by selecting Stock Setup under Warehouse Tracking.
  • Scan each item in the quantities that reflect your inventory. When you have fully scanned all of your inventory (or a distinct sub-set).
  • Go to File | Load Original Inventory | Import from Barcode Scanner.
    • From this screen, loading the inventory is a three-step process:
    1. First, click Import Data to pull the scanned items from your device.
      • Select the scanner type and click Import.
      • The status bar at the bottom of the form should reflect the records received.
      • Click Close from the Import form.
    2. Second, click Compare Inventory to generate the discrepancy report from the newly imported data. You are now ready to import the new levels.
    3. Finally, click Import Discrepancies. You will be prompted for the newly created discrepancy report:


      • Select the most current file (check date) in the Invent folder
      • Click the Open button.
      • Wait until the software prompts you that the Import procedure is complete and how many items were imported.

  • Your new stock quantities should be reflected in your database. You now will only have to add the stock in the warehouse, if applicable.
  • Click Menu to return to the Main Menu.

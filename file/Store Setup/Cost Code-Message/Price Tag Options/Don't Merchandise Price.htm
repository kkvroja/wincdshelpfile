Don't Merchandise Price

By default, Custom Design Software will merchandise On Sale and Factory List Prices depending on rounding.  All prices will be merchandise to 5.50 or 9.95.  Therefore, a price with the last dollar digits ending in 8 would mathematically change to 9.95.  A price ending in 2 would mathematically change to 5.50.

Examples:

  •     Price  $848.34 would be changes to $849.95. 

  •     Price $122.38 would change to $125.50

If you don't want your prices merchandised or you want your own special endings, uncheck this option.  You then can make your own price endings.

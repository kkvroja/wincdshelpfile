Commissions

If you pay a separate commission on after market products such as Stain Protection, enter the commission rate in the box.  Do not use the percentage sign.

If you pay commission on Delivery Charges, Labor Charges or Notes, check the appropriate boxes and Click Apply to save the changes. 

If you pay commissions based on Gross Margin, you need to set up the commission table as well as setting the slider at the bottom on the form.  To pay commission on Delivered Sales, by Gross Margin, for example, move the Slider to No 2, Click on Paid on Delivered and fill in the range from lowest to highest as in our sample.  Make sure to enter the commission for each range.  Click Apply to save.

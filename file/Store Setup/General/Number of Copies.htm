Number of Copies

At the left, bottom corner of the form, you can select how many copies of the Bill Of Sale you want printed for each sale. When you get the software, it will be set to 1 copy. After you enter all your Inventory, the next job is to enter all your sales that are undelivered at the time. During this period, you only need one copy of the Bill Of Sale.

When you are up and running, you would want at least 2 copies printed. The sales are printed in this order:

  • File Copy
  • Customers Receipt
  • Delivery Copy
  • Sales Persons copy

Company Identifier

Used to uniquely identify a credit grantor. Report your internal code to identify each branch, office, and/or credit central where information is verified. This number must be unique and at least 5 digits long. Entire field should never be zero, blank, or 9 filled.

This field must be consistent on a month-to- month basis to avoid duplication of information. Notify credit reporting agencies before adding, deleting, or changing the identifiers in this field.

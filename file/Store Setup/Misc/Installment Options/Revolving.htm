Revolving Options

Revolving accounts are essentially store credit accounts.

In practice, Revolving Accounts are created and managed much like Installment Accounts, with a few important differences. Interest is added and compounded on a monthly statement. The Same as Cash option applies to each sale in a revolving account.

To create a Revolving Account, use the Store Finance option when selecting payment method on a new sale, and enter a finance account number ending in " R".

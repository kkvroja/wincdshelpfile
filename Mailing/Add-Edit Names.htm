Add/Edit Names


To view or change information in the customer database, click Mailing: Add/Edit Names. An "Add & Edit Names" form will appear. Enter the customer's telephone number, and click OK. Alternatively, to search by name, click the Name radio box, type the customer's last name, and click OK. Any changes made in here are saved in the database when you click OK.  You can also enter customers that have not purchased from you before.

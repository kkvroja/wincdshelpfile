A/R Ageing Report

To run the Ageing Report click Installment, Reports, A/R Ageing Report. An "Ageing Reports" form will appear. Choose to sort either by Name or by Account Number, then click Print or Print Preview.

The A/R Ageing Report will show all open contracts. Customers Name, telephone and ageing.  Your Credit Manager can call customers directly from this report. 

At the bottom of the Ageing Reports you can find totals for 30, 60,90 day accounts and also the percentage.

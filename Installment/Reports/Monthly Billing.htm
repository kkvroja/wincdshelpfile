Monthly Billing

Since our software provides coupon books to be given to the customer at the Point Of Sale, it is really unnecessary to send a monthly statements.  Using the coupon books saves a tremendous amount of money in postage and labor alone. 

If you feel you have to print statements, Click on Installments, Reports, Monthly Billing.

Statements are run on the 1st., 10th., and the 20th.  A self addressed statement will be printed for each account if needed.

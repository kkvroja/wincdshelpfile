Payment Estimator

In a Credit Store operation, one of the most important jobs is to be able to tell a customer "How Much Per Month".  To estimate payments, click Installment: Payment Estimator. Alternatively, if you choose Store Financing when writing a new sale, this form will appear.

At the top, you will see two date fields. Use the top one to choose the delivery date, and the bottom one to choose when the first payment is due.  This is not necessary just to compute the payment. 

  • Previous Balance refers to a customer's previous existing contract. This is used as an add-on sale (to add new items to an existing contract).

  • Gross Sale with Tax refers to the new items that the customer is purchasing.

  • Original Deposit is the total amount the customer is putting down on the sale, including COD.

  • Documentation Fee is pre-set to your store's requirements.

  • Life, Accident, and Property Insurance, will pop up automatically if you sell Insurance and the formulas are set up in advance or entered manually.  If you do not want any insurance, uncheck the insurance box.  The payment will automatically recalculate.

  • Months to Finance defaults to 12 months. This can be changed as needed. 

  • Amount to Finance is automatically calculated.

  • Finance Charge is automatically calculated.

  • Payment will be is automatically calculated.

Note: Always use the Tab button to move between fields (use Shift-Tab to move backwards between fields). This will ensure that the fields are automatically updated.

To use "Same as Cash" financing, choose the number of months in the Cash Opt. pull down box located across from Property Insurance.  It defaults to 0 months.

Click Print to print a contract for the customer. If you have Print Coupons Option set, choose Monthly or Weekly coupons.

The above procedure is the same when setting up a sale with Installment Financing.

Edit Accounts

To view payments, click Installment: Edit Accounts. An "Installment Customer" form will appear. Enter the customer's Account Number, or choose Telephone or Name and enter the Telephone number or Last Name, then choose OK.

Use edit accounts to Credit or Debit customers for such things as Late Charges, and Insurance adjustments and you can even change the terms of the contract.  If you are adjusting a late charge, then the amount gets entered under Late Charges.  Click Apply to post the change.  All other entries are entered in the payment entry because the get adjusted from the principal. 

When the account is paid off or reopened, the Status is automatically changed to O for Open or C for closed.  If you are writing off the account, you can change the status to W.  This will prevent accounts from getting statements, Late Charges and show up on reports.  The account can be re-opened if the customer begins to pay.

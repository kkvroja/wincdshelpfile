Void Contract

To void contracts, click Installment, Void Contract. An "Installment Customer" form will appear.
Enter the customer's Account Number, or choose Telephone or Name and enter the Telephone number or Last Name, then choose OK.

A message box will appear on top of the "Customer Account Card" form confirming that you want to void this contract.  Click Apply and the contract will Void out and not be reusable.

Loading Original Inventory

Make out the master department list. (See Departmentalizing the Inventory). After you install the software on all computers, 

  1. Add every item in stock (Adding New Inventory), but enter 0 for Quantity and Stock in Loc. 1).
  2. After all items have been entered, print out a Physical Inventory Worksheet (Inventory: Reports: Inventory Reports, click Sort, choose Sort by Vendor, and click Sort. Then, choose Physical Worksheet, and Print).
  3. Use the sheets that printed out to perform a physical inventory for all items in the store. Use the W column for Warehouse, F for Floor, and T (optionally) if you put inventory on tags.
  4. After all items have been counted, print a Manufacturers List (Inventory: Reports: Manufacturers List). The Manufacturers List will show all manufacturers and their corresponding 3-digit numbers. Use these 3-digit numbers to bring up each manufacturer by number when loading the original inventory.
  5. Go to File: Load Original Inventory to enter the quantities.
  6. From the Physical Worksheet, enter the total quantity (for all stores) in the Quantity box. Then, enter the stock for each location below the Quantity box for each respective location. As you enter stock for each location, the computer will automatically totals the sum in the Allocation box. When the Available box equals the Allocation box, click the OK button to proceed to the next item, and repeat this step.
    Note: The Allocation must equal Quantity before you can continue.

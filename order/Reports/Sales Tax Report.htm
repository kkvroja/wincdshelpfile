Sales Tax Report



The Sales Tax Report is used to prepare your sales tax for the state at the end of the month.

The Sales Tax Report shows all orders in the date range.

To print or view a Sales Tax Report, Go to the Order Entry Menu and go to the Reports section. Select Sales Tax Report (or press Shift-F3).

A Sales Tax Report form will appear. Enter the date range for the month needed, and choose Delivered or Written sales depending on your State requirements. Next, choose either Print or Print Preview.

Print will print the report on the default printer.
Print Preview will show the report on screen.

For help with the Print Preview function, see the Print Preview help page.

At the end of the report, it will show a gross sales with tax and Non taxable sales, and a separate tax total.

If you deliver to different Sales Tax areas, a separate sales tax report for each area will be printed. These are predefined in the Store Setup area.

Customer Service Module

When a customer calls with a service complaint, click Service Module on the Order Entry menu. Enter the customer's telephone number on the form and press Enter.. Every item the customer ever bought at your store appears on the screen.

The data shows Vendor, Style Number, Bill of Sale Number, Quantity, Delivery Date, and Description. From the customers description, you can easily ascertain which item has the problem. Click on the item and it changes color to blue.

Click on the red button marked Tag For Repair. The line item turns red signifying that it is tagged for repair.

If you want to have an extra note attached to the line item such as "Sold Off Floor", click the button, Add Note to Item. This will open a large box so you can type in any extra notes. The note will have the time and date stamped automatically.


Check any of the four service options as appropriate. This will give quick recognition of what is to be done on the service call. This one is checked Store Service Enter the customer's complaint in the Customer's Complaint box. If you want to setup a service date, use the Date Drop Down at the top of the service form. Enter a service description in the Store Response box.

Saving and Printing

Click Save to save the service order. Click Print to print out a copy of the service call. The software will automatically fill in the Service Order No. box. Click Next to proceed to the next service call, or Main Menu to return to the main menu. Note: You can continually add to the same Service Call as the saga continues. If you have to order parts or make a return trip, just call up the same service call by phone number or by service call number. To close the service call after it is completed, put the service call on the screen and click on the Status dropdown. Select Closed. Click Save. This removes the service call from the Open Service Call Report.

Customer Lookup

To create a new sale, click New Sales from the Order Entry menu. A "Check for Prior Sales" form will appear. Enter the customer's telephone number in the following format: 3024933842. It will be automatically formatted as you type.

Customer Information

Choose Ok to continue.

If this is a new customer, a message box will show up asking "Name not in database: Try again?". If you entered the wrong phone number, choose Yes; otherwise, choose No to continue.

Delivery or Pick-up

If the customer's record is found, the next step would be to set the customer up for Delivery or Pick-Up, if necessary. Click on the Delivery check box. A calendar will open up to allow you to select the delivery or pick up date.

NOTE: If the customer is taking the item with them at the time of sale, or it is a special order, skip this step.

It will default to None. The date can be set later when the Special Order is received or the customer is ready for delivery. See, Inventory, Check Order Status to set up a later delivery or pick up date. Checking the Delivery of Pick-Up check box DOES NOT DELIVER THE MERCHANDISE. It just sets up the delivery date in the Delivery Calendar, Delivery Load Sheets and Delivery Tickets!

Status

The status below the Sale No. always defaults to "New" on a New Sale under order entry.

When you go to View Sale under Order Entry, that Status below the sale number changes depending on the status the sale is in and this tells the store manager/salesperson the status of the sale.

All sales first go to Open status on the View Sale (Except if its a Lay A Way sale). Now, any sale other than Lay A Way or StoreFinanced will show up as Open initially.


The next status is normally Delivered or Voided depending on what is done with the sale.

A delivered sale would show up with Delivered status



Ex: If the sale type was Lay Way Quote, it will display such

Other Information

If you need to enter any special instructions for the drivers, such as delivery time of cross streets, click into Special Instructions and type in what is needed. You can use up to 3 lines of information. As you tab off the Special Instructions area, the Sales Staff form automatically appears. Double click a sales person and their name will be added to Sales 1. If there is a split sale, the software will handle up to a three way split. The commissions will be split automatically in the commission program when run. If all information is correct, click the apply button to continue. If the delivery is being made to a different address, use the Ship To area. We also can track the E-Mail address.

Saving the Information

After the sale is saved, you can Double Click on the E-Mail address and the software automatically jumps into your e-mail browser.

Note: Only use the Ship To address if the delivery is going to a different address. This will eliminate confusion.

Pricing and Advertizing

Under Status, there are two blank Dropdown boxes. Leave these blank by default. If the customer receives special pricing, or is a wholesale customer, choose the appropriate pricing option in the first box. Pricing is as follows: The prices that come up on the order are calculated over Landed Cost.
  • Customer: on sale price
  • Resale 1: 10% over landing cost
  • Resale 2: 15% over landing cost
  • Resale 3: 20% over landing cost
The second drop down contains several user-definable categories. You can use these categories for the mailing list, to determine which advertisements bring in the most customers.



Examples of use might be:
  • TV
  • Mail
  • Radio
  • s Word of Mouth

Tax Dropdown

The Tax drop down lets you pre-setup the customer who is outside you default sales tax zone. This will be saved in the database for each time he purchases something from the store. If you have the software set in the Store Set Up to manually add your own Bill Of Sales numbers, then type in the correct Bill Of Sale number.

Note: This is help full when you first start the software, and are entering you old Bill Of Sales into the system. You can enter the customer's original sale number.

Print Label

The Print Label button is used in conjunction with the Dymo Label printer. If you want an address label for an envelope, just click the button.

Body of sale

Finally, if everything is correct on the top half of the order, click OK and the main body of the sale appears.

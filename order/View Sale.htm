View Sale

Opening A sale

To view a sale that has already been written:
  1. Go to Order Entry | View Sale (or press Ctrl-V from the Main Menu).
  2. An empty Bill of Sale will be displayed and a box will appear asking for the sale number to view. Type in the Bill of Sale number.
  3. Press Enter.
The Bill of Sale will show on the screen.
If you desire to see all of the customer information, click on the store logo or address at the top of the page.
To return to the line-item view, click Show Body Of Sale at the bottom of the page.

Navigating To Other Sales

On the right side, use the Up and Dn buttons to cycle through Bill of Sales.
To view another sale without returning to the Main Menu, click Next Sale.

Other View Sale Options

Print CopyClick the print button (a small printer at the right side of the screen) to print a copy of that sale.
Email CopyIf a valid email address has been entered into the customer information and you are properly connected to the internet, you may click the blue @ sign at the right edge of the screen to email a copy of the BoS to the customer. The 'From' address will be the store email address you have entered in Store Setup. If either of these email address are not properly entered, you will be notified.
View/Add Sale Notes Here you can view your sale notes.
Use the box at the bottom to add a new note. The next time you view the note, it will be time-and-date stamped. Also, when you view the sale on the screen, you will be notified if a note has been written in the past 30 days.

Put information in notes such as:
  • Customer promised payment in one week.
  • Called customer, left message on voice mail.

Converting Lay-A-Way Sales

If you are using Lay-A-Way sales and have a sale you wish to convert, you can convert any line item with a status of LAW to PO (Tagged incomming stock), SO (Special Order), or ST (Stock).
To convert a Lay-A-Way sale while viewing a sale, simply double click the status of the line you wish to convert. You should see the following dialog box:



Select the what you would like this item to be converted to and click OK (or Cancel to make no change). The sale and inventory will be updated to reflect the change.

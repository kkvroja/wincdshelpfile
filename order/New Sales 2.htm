The Body of the Sale

After entering the customer's information and clicking Ok, the form Click on Selection appears.

Enter Style Number

  • Enter Style Number

    • Choose this option to enter the items on the sale whether stock or Special Order.  An Enter Style Number form will appear. If you know the style number, you can type it in, and choose OK (or press Enter).  If you are not sure of the style number, click on the center button Desc which is short for Description.  The entire description will open up in the help window.  You can double click on the style number in the help window to bring up the item. 

      If you don't know the style number, choose Vendor Name. A vendor list box will appear. Double-click the appropriate vendor and a new list containing style numbers and descriptions appears. Find the correct item from the description and Double-click the appropriate item.

      If the item sold is in the Inventory database, then the form Choose Status will appear. Enter the quantity of this item that the customer is buying, choose which location the inventory will be taken from, it will default to how it is set up in store setup. 

      For more information about selecting inventory items with this option, view the next page, Choose Quantity & Status.

  • Stain Protection

    • If your store charges stain protection on upholstered pieces, choose Stain Protection, then OK. Tab over to price and enter the appropriate value.

  • Delivery Charge

    • If your store charges for delivery, choose Delivery Charge, then OK. Tab over to price and enter the appropriate value.
      Note: If this charge is taxable, the option must be changed in File, Store Set Up, Options. This option was preset for you based on your Order Form.

  • Labor Charge

    • If your store charges for any type of labor services, choose Labor Charge, then OK. Tab over to price and enter the appropriate value.

    • Note: If this charge is taxable, the option must be changed in File, Store Set Up, Options. This option was preset for you based on your Order Form.

  • Tax 1

    • Click on  Tax 1, the default state sales tax. The software will automatically subtotal, and calculate tax for this order. Always Tab Off the price field to proceed.

  • - Tax 1

    • Click on - Tax 1 to calculate the sale with the default sales tax backed out. The software will automatically subtotal, and calculate tax for this order. Always Tab Off the price field to proceed.

  • Variable Tax

    • If you deliver to cities/states with different sales tax rates, click on the List, and choose the appropriate tax necessary for this sale. Choose OK. The software will automatically subtotal, and calculate tax for this order. Tab off Tax to proceed.  The variable sales tax was set up for you in Store Set Up.

  • - Variable Tax

    • As with - Tax 1, click this to calculate the sale with a variable tax rate backed out. Choose the appropriate tax necessary for this sale. Choose OK. The software will automatically subtotal, and calculate tax for this order. Tab off Tax to proceed.
      The variable sales tax was set up for you in Store Set Up.

  • Notes

    • There are two uses for Notes.  First, to use notes if more room is needed for Description when doing a Special Order. After the Special Order item is added, Click Notes.  To add a note to a Purchase Order, the trick is to include the manufacturer's name under the Manufacturer field. Then, in Description, add any necessary details about the special order (such as "Blue fabric" or "Mahogany." This information will show up when the PO is printed/faxed. Secondly, if the Manufacturer field is left blank, the note can be used for store notes such as "Final Sale" or "Sold As-Is."

  • Purchase Gift Card

    • Choose this option to purchase a gift card. Tab off to proceed.

  • Carpet
    • For carpet orders, a carpet yardage calculator will appear, followed by a style selection. Enter the appropriate values and click OK. Tab off to proceed.

      For more information about selecting inventory items with this option, view the next page, Choose Quantity & Status.

  • Payment

    • After the Sales Tax is added, you generally need to enter a deposit.  Click on the Payment option. A list with payment types will appear. Choose the appropriate type (such as Cash or Visa), then OK. Enter the amount paid with this type of payment. If there are multiple types of payment given, repeat this step for each payment.

    • If the customer will be using In-Store Financing, enter after any deposits, click Store Financing, then OK. The "Payment Setup" form will appear. See Installment Module for more help.

Deleting an Item



To delete a line entry on a new sale, click the gray box to left edge of the corresponding line ou wish to delete (the line will appear highlighted), and press the Delete key. The line will be deleted, and the balance will recalculate. 

Discarding the Sale

If you wish to discard an entire sale , click Clear button at the bottom of the page.  This will clear the entire page. Alternatively, you can click the Main Menu button to exit without processing the sale to discard the current sale.

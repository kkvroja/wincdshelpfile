Credit Card Manager

From this form, you can make perform Credit Card maintenance functions such as doing voids and returns.

Processing Credit/Debit Card Returns

To make a void or a return, select the operation you wish to perform (NOTE: Credit cards and Debit cards are processed separately, so use the appropriate transaction for the operation you wish to use). Once you have selected the operation you wish to use, enter the amount of the purchase.

When you have entered all the required information, click on Execute to process the result.

Obtaining a Transaction Report

The last option for transaction type is to obtain a report from the credit card processor. Selecting this option will retrieve the report and display it. When you are done with the report, double click anywhere on it and it will disappear. If you wish to print the report, you will be prompted at that time.

Exiting

When you are done, click on Menu to exit the form.

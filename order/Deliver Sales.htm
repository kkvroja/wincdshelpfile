Deliver Sales


Selecting the sale to be Delivered

After a customer's order has been physically delivered or picked up, go to Order Entry | Delivery Sales (Ctrl-D). You must complete this section because the merchandise has to be removed from the Inventory database. To proceed, click on Deliver Sale and enter the Bill of Sale number, then press Enter.


Delivering the Line Items

The Deliver Sale form will appear. If you have to Back Date the date to the day of delivery, use the drop down calendar. For each item on that sale, choose either Deliver or Skip. If the truck brings back an item from the delivery, then it is not delivered so you click the Skip button instead of the Deliver button.

Be sure to delivery everything on the page including the payments and subtotals etc. This will make it easier to spot a Back Ordered Item later.



Entering Final Payments (with receipts/email available)

Because you're in the delivery section, the software proceeds to the payment form automatically. Enter the payment amount and type (listed in the How Pd. Dropdown Box), then click OK.



  • Options:
    • Checking Receipt will print out a receipt to mail back to the customer.
    • Checking Email will email a copy of the new Bill of Sale to the customer's email address on file.
 Tip: Use Memo for check or credit card information, or any other pertinent information.



Partial Payment on Delivery

Often, the customer does not pay the full balance, but leaves a partial balance. Say an item was damaged and they want to withhold $50.00 or so. When a partial payment is made in delivery section, the software automatically displays the following message:

Cannot Finish -- Balance Still Due
There is still a balance due. Please enter the payment amount or change the payment type to Back Order, Outside
Finance Company, or Store Finance.

When you click OK, the payment amount is cleared. Select Backorder, Outside Finance Company, or Store Finance in the How Pd. dropdown box, then click OK (or press Enter).
The software then allows you to finish delivering the sale but sets up the remaining balance due as a receivable.

In Store Financing

If your store does in-store financing, and you're setting this up as a financed contract for the balance due, select Store Finance in the How Pd. dropdown box. When you click OK, the software will immediately jump in to the installment setup module. See Financing Module for help.

Outside Finance Company

Select Outside Finance Company if the contract was sold to an outside financing agency.
If the contract was sold to an outside finance company, when you receive payment, go to Customer Payments and pay off the bill in full regardless if the outside finance company charged a fee.
If a fee was incurred with the finance company, enter the fee under Order Entry | Cash Drawer Entries

Creating a Backorder Sale

Backorder means there is still a partial balance due.
To create a backordered sale, simply select the Back Order option from the list and click OK. This will change the status of the sale to Back Order and finish the delivery process.

If an item was backordered, and there is a small balance due on it, when the transaction is complete, go back to Deliver Sale. Deliver the piece that was delivered, and pay off the balance due when the Payment form appears.

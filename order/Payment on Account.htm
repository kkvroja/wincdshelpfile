Payment on Account


Posting a Payment

To post a payment on a customer's order for a Lay-a-Way payment or such, click Payment on Account in the Order Entry menu. Enter the Bill of Sale number, then press Enter. A "Payment" form will appear.

Enter the payment amount and type (listed in the How Pd. Dropdown Box), then click Ok.

If the sale is paid off, you can also check the Pay All box.

Note: Clicking Receipt will print out a receipt as well as post the payment.

Tip: Use Memo for check or credit card number, or any other pertinent information.

Reverse Payment

Reverse Payment on sales may be accomplished by entering the same payment with a minus sign (-) in front of the amount. For example, -100.00. This will reverse the payment if there is an error on a NSF check.

Cash Register

Custom Design Software offers a optional interface to do Cash & Carry Sales similar to a department store cash register. NOTE: To use this sales interface, you need a POS Printer and a keyboard wedge barcode scanner.
NOTE: We also sell a cash drawer which opens as the sale is made (see our price list). This interface is extremely useful if you sell a lot of accessories and customers bring these items to the counter.

Starting the Cash Register interface

To open the Cash Register (for Cash & Carry sales only), click Cash Register on the Order Menu. The Cash Register form will open.

Settting the default POS Printer (first time only)s

The first time you use the Cash Register interface, you need to set the default POS printer. From the printer list, find the POS Printer, (use the slider for more printer options) and click on the printer. Next, click on Always Use This Printer and the printer option will be saved. Your logo or customer address will appear in the printer setup area.

NOTE: If you ever need to change your default POS Printer, double click on the address area and the printer options will reappear.

To use the Cash Register, scan the first item with the scanner (you can also type in the style number). The following form will appear to allow you to enter the quantity:


If the quantity of 1 is Ok, click or press Apply. If the customer bought more than one item, type over the 1 and enter the correct quantity. The computer will re-calculate the sale. Repeat this for any additional items. If any items are to be removed from the sale, click on Return and the item will be taken off the sale. After the last item is selected, click on Payment and the payment options will be available:



Click on the payment method and the final payment screen appears:

For credit cards, if not using our integrated credit card system, the default card type is Visa. To change, click the dropdown and select the appropriate card. Click Apply to post the payment.

Finishing the Sale

On the final screen, click on Finish Sale which prints out the receipt. If you want to leave the software in this Cash Register mode, leave the screen alone. Otherwise, click on Menu to return to the Main Menu.

Next Sale

When you click on Next Sale the screen clears and you are ready for the next sale. Note: There is no duplicate receipt printed for the store. The Cash & Carry sale creates its own normal Bill Of Sale and may be viewed under View Sale on the Order Entry menu. All inventory is immediately reduced and delivered in the software.

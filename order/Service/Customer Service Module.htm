Customer Service Module

When a customer calls with a service complaint, click Order Entry | Service Module, Service and enter the customer's telephone number on the form.

Selecting Items for Service

Every item the customer ever bought at your store appears on the screen. The data shows Vendor, Style Number, Bill of Sale Number, Quantity, Delivery Date, and Description.

From the customers description, you can easily ascertain which item has the problem. Click on the item and its changes color to blue. Click on the red button marked Tag For Repair. The line item turns red signifying that it is tagged for repair.



If you want to have an extra note attached to the line item such as "Sold Off Floor", click the button, Add Note to Item.  This will open a large box so you can type in any extra notes. The note will be marked with the date automatically.



Service Type

Check any of the four service options as appropriate:
  • Store Service
  • Outside Service
  • Pick Up Exchange
  • Other
This will give quick recognition of what is to be done on the service call.  This one is checked Store Service

Complaint and Response

Enter the customer's complaint in the Customer's Complaint box. If you want to setup a service date, use the Date Drop Down at the top of the service form. Enter a service description in the Store Response box.

Save Service Order

You must click the Save button in order to save your changes. If you don't, any changes will be lost. Click Save to save the service order.
Click Print to print out a copy of the service call. The software will automatically fill in the Service Order No. box.
Click Next to proceed to the next service call, or Main Menu to return to the main menu.

Note: You can continually add to the same Service Call as the correspondence continues. If you have to order parts or make a return trip, just call up the same service call by phone number or by number and continue managing it.

Ordering Parts

If you have parts ordered or on order, there will be a status indicator in the lower right by the Order Parts button. If you want to order parts, click on the button in the lower right, and you will be brought to the Order Parts screen.

Closing a Service Call

To close the service call after it is completed, load the call and click on the Status dropdown. Select Closed. Click Save. This removes the service call from the Open Service Call Report.

Exiting

To exit the Service Module, click Menu.

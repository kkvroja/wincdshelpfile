Customer Stock Preview

Often times, you loose sales because you don't have a certain piece in stock. Use Customer Stock Preview to show the customer the missing piece. If you keep a computer on the sales floor, this is where employees can look up merchandise, and also verify that they are selling the right piece.

Viewing Stock

Click Customer Stock Preview from the Order Entry menu. Enter the Style number in the box for it and tab off. If there is a picture available for this piece, it will appear on the screen. Alternatively, you can click Select Item By Style to bring up a form to help you find the style number you wish to find. This will bring up the standard select style box for you to either enter a partial style, select from a vendor list, or browse by descriptions.



TIP: The screen can be resized for better view of the image. Note, however, if you use a low-quality digital image, it may appear grainy as it is stretched past its original dimensions.

TIP: Use a digital camera, not a scanner, to capture the pictures, because you capture only the specific piece using a digital camera instead of the group image. (The factory photos usually include all related pieces in the group)

Printing the Piece

You can click the print button to print the image with pricing information. You can use this as a quote for the customer.

Displaying Inventory

There is also a blank button above Select Item By Style. Clicking this button will show you the inventory available at all locations, and total inventory available for sale. If you click this button again, this data will disappear.

Returning to the Main Menu

To return to the Main Menu, click Close.

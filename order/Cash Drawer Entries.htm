Cash Drawer Entries

Custom Design Software completely controls your bookkeeping and all journals. The Cash Drawer option under Order Entry is where you make your bank deposits and also controls you petty cash entries.

Making An Entry

To make a Cash Drawer Entry, click Cash Drawer Entries on the Order Menu. Money comes into the cash drawer through Customer Deposits. This is entered automatically as you make payments. Very little else comes into the cash drawer.



Click the Cash In or Cash Out dropdown box, select the appropriate item (the account number is automatically filled-in when you choose an item), enter the amount, set the date, and click Post (or press Enter).

Other Money

For other money coming in, click Cash In, for example:
  • Medical Co-Pay if an employee pays for his/her medical co-pay with cash, instead of as a payroll deduction.
  • Finance Co. Rebate if your finance company gives you a rebate.
  • Misc. Cash for any other transactions.
Example of Cash Out:
  • Gas & Oil to pay for gas for the store's delivery truck.
  • Meals & Entertainment to pay for lunch for the store.
  • Banking Deposits must be entered for the day.
Note: When the Banking Module is setup, there is an option under Store Set Up which when set, will automatically post the bank deposits into the Banking Module.

Petty Cash Journal

The petty cash journal is included as a section of the Daily Audit Report. If there are any more entries to post in the Cash Drawer, click Next or click Cancel.

Email POs



To send POs via Email, you must first set up your email system. This is done in the Email Config Panel in the Utilities section of the File Menu.

You will also have to enter your store's return email address in the box provided.

Once this is done, you can specify the PO range by one of two methods:
  1. Enter the PO Date range
  2. Enter the PO # range
If you would like to print the PO as well as email it, click Print PO As Well? to do so.
If you have already emailed them, and wish to email them again for some reason, click Re-Send Email? to reprint the POs specified.

When entered, click Email POs. A results panel will appear to the right.

Click Close when finished.

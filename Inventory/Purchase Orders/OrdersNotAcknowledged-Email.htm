Factory Email - Orders Not Acknowledged

Use this panel to email the factory of Orders Not Acknowleged.

use the date on the left to pick the date to use. Then, select the POs on the right using the checkboxes to select the POs you wish to email about. POs without a factory email address will have an asterisk (*) before them and will not be automatically selected. They cannot be emailed even if checked.

Click Email to send the emails through your current email profile.

Ashley EDI ATP - Item Alignment

Configuration



The first time into the Ashley Panel, you will be prompted to configure your Ashley Direct login. Enter your "External Company ID" and "Keycode", both exactly as given by your Ashley representitive.

When you are done, click OK to save the details.

Adding An Item



When you enter the Ashley Panel, the item you were just viewing is already selected into the "Add Item" panel. Select the type of inquiry you would like to make and the options for that item, and click "Add".

Main Panel



Fom this this screen, you can either add more items, or click Request to submit your inquriy.

If you are done, you can click Exit to close this panel.

Login



The first time you click Request, the software will ask you for your username and password. This information will be saved if you successfully log in so long as the panel is still open. You will need to re-enter this information each time you come to the panel in order to keep your account information secure.

Click Enter to submit the request.

Communicating



After you submit your query with your login information, the system will communicate with Ashley Direct to provide your results. Please be patient as your request is being processed.

Results



This display shows the results of your inquiry.

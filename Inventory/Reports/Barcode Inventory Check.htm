Mini Scanner Inventory Check

Overview

Use this to check the current store stock levels of particular items with the Mini Scanner.

Performing Inventory

  1. First, scan the items you want to check stock on.
  2. Next, plug the scanner in.
  3. Finally, from the inventory menu, click on Barcode Inventory Check.

Stock Levels

Click Ok and the software will display the On Sale, List Price, the Stock Quantity, and On Order at each location. Click Done to clear the screen.

NOTE: In this mode, the items in the Mini Scanner do not get cleared.

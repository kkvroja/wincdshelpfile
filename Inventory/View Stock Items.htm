View Stock Items



Checking Inventory

To check inventory and other details, click Inventory: View Stock Items. An "Enter Style Number" form will appear. Enter a style number, or click Vendor Name, and select from the list.

The screen will show the details and inventory allocations for this item. Use Scan Up or Scan Dn to cycle through items.

Tags and Purchase Orders

Use the Print Tags and PO button to print new tags or generate a purchase order, respectively.

Other Options

Click Next to enter a new style number, or chose a new manufacturer.
Click Print to print an exact copy of the page.
To delete an item from the database, Click Delete.

NOTE: To delete an item, you must have 0 stock and 0 on order for stock. If you accidentally delete an item, you can recapture the item in Restore Deleted Records.

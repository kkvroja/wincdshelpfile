Multi-Store Billing

Overview

In a Multi Store chain, the warehouse is always shipping merchandise to other stores and to other stores customer. Using the Multi-Store Billing report, you can actually bill the other stores for the merchandise you deliver to their customers.

The software starts at location 1 and prints bills out to stores 2,3,4 etc. Then it starts at store 2 and bills to stores 1,3,4 etc. in case there was merchandise delivered from other locations.

Printing

To use this, set the date range and click Print.

Multi-Store Transfer Billing

When you do a store transfer for stock merchandise in a Multi-Store chain, you have to be able to bill the stores for the merchandise shipped to them for Stock.

The software starts at location 1 and prints bills out to stores 2, 3, 4, etc. Then it starts at store 2 and bills to stores 1, 3, 4 etc. in case there was stock merchandise moved from other locations.

Simply set the date range and click Print. You can select all stores or each store individually in the drop down.

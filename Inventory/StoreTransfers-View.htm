Transfer and Void

Once you have scheduled a transfer, it must be either completed or voided.

Selecting a Transfer



You will be prompted for a transfer number.
Either enter the transfer number or select from the lookup to the right. The transfers will be sorted by Transfer Number and will show the current status (TP = Pending Transfer, TR = Completed Transfer, TV = Voided Transfer).

Transfering And Voiding Items



Once you have your transfer on the screen, you can either Void or Transfer each line. Simply highlight the line you wish to finish, and click either Void or Transfer at the top.

Transfer/Void All

To transfer or void all lines that are still pending, click either Transfer All or Void All.

NOTE: Only once you transfer a line will the Stock quantities be changed, so be sure to record which transfers have been completed.

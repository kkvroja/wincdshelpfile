Inventory Scanner Keypad

The keypad of the 8061-C Bluetooth scanner allows you to type in both letters, numbers, and most common symbols. To save space, these keys are combined onto fewer buttons. When you first power on the unit, pressing the keys will result in the symbol in white being entered. You can also type in either captial or small letters, as well as put the keypad into function mode. To type the alternate letters/symbols (the ones in yellow), press the FN/Alpha key at the bottom right of the keypad until you see the appropriate symbol in the bottom left of the screen. A represents capital letters, a represents lower-case letters, F represents Function Mode, and none indicating normal input.

Once you have entered a value, you can press either of the two blue buttons which both act as Enter keys.

If you would like to scan a value into the scanner instead of entering it into the keypad, aim the scanner at the barcode you would like to scan and press the large, yellow button at the top center of the keypad.

If you are on a form with multiple fields, you can generally use the up and down buttons to select the field you wish to enter into next.

If you are on a menu, you can either use the up and down arrows to select an item and press one of the Blue enter keys or press the number corresponding to that menu item to automatically select it for ease of use.

At almost any time, you can press the ESC key to exit out of any menu, form, or process.

Inventory Scanner

We make use of CipherLAB's line of Bluetooth scanners. The scanners make use of bluetooth technology to provide you with wireless access to your computer's database with good range and reliability. You can get started using our wireless scanners or purchase additional scanners or access points by contacting us!
With the inventory scanner, you can perform basic Warehouse Tracking functions, Item Lookup, or take a physical inventory of a location. These features are arranged into three menus for ease of use with an additional Utility/Configuration menu.

  • Item Lookup
  • Physical Inventory

Physical Inventory - Inventory Scanner

There are options in the Physical Inventory Menu:
  • Scan
  • Upload Data
  • Delete Data

Scan

To scan an item, first select Scan from the Physical Inventory menu. Enter the location where you are performing your inventory (for example, press 1 on the keypad and press the Blue Enter key). Next, scan a barcode and enter the quantity. Continue scanning and entering quantities until you are done. Press ESC when you are done scanning.

Upload Data

When you are finished uploading data, you can use the Upload Data option to upload it to WinCDS. See the relevant data import section for how to prepare WinCDS to receive the data.

Delete Recs

To delete the records without uploading them to WinCDS, select the third option. To confirm this operation, you will be prompted to enter 123 into the field presented and press enter. Pressing Enter without entering 123or ESC will cancel the delete.
